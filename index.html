About us

Team Sandviken 2008 is a basketball team from Bergen, Norway's second-largest city on the west coast. The team's home arena is Stemmemyren, just north of Bergen city center.
Most of the players have known each other since starting at Hellen Primary School at the age of 6. Originally, they played on the club's football team but switched to basketball around the age of 12. As they transitioned to secondary school, the team was strengthened by new classmates from Rothaugen Secondary School.
The teams greatest strength is their team spirit, knowing each other like brothers.
